Everything you need to know

At Venture X, we provide a comprehensive suite of amenities designed for modern professionals:

  • High-speed fiber internet and secure Wi-Fi
  • Fully equipped meeting rooms with video conferencing capabilities
  • Premium coffee bar and refreshment station
  • 24/7 secure building access
  • Professional-grade printing and scanning services
  • Comfortable lounges and collaborative spaces
  • Dedicated phone booths for private calls
  • Mail and package handling services
  • Regular networking events and community activities

Booking a workspace at Venture X is simple and flexible:

  1. Choose your preferred workspace option from our Solutions section
  2. For immediate bookings: Select your desired option and complete the booking process online
  3. For custom solutions: Schedule a tour to visit our locations and discuss your specific needs
  4. For day passes: Book directly through our website or walk in during business hours
  5. For meeting rooms: Use our online booking system to reserve your preferred time slot

Our team is always available to help you find the perfect workspace solution for your needs.

  • Full access to both facilities during business hours
  • Ability to book meeting rooms at either location

  • Flexibility to work from whichever location is most convenient for you

This dual-location access allows you to maximize your productivity across the Lehigh Valley.

We offer various flexible membership plans to suit different working styles:

  • Shared Desks ($300/month): First-come-first-served workspace access
  • Dedicated Desks ($500/month): Your personal workspace in our community
  • Private Offices ($850/month): Secure, furnished private space
  • Virtual Office ($100/month): Professional business address and mail handling
  • Day Passes ($30/day): Flexible daily access
  • Meeting Rooms ($20/hour): Professional meeting spaces as needed

All plans include access to our core amenities and community benefits.
